# LLM Wiki

An LLM Wiki is a markdown-based knowledge base that stores source-backed knowledge as linked pages rather than leaving information trapped in chats, emails, videos, or loose documents.

In Dimitris's setup, the LLM Wiki lives inside the Obsidian vault and complements [[concepts/hermes-memory|Hermes Memory]]. Hermes can ingest a source, save the raw material, create or update pages, add `[[wikilinks]]`, and keep an index/log so the knowledge base improves over time.

## Why it matters

The core idea from the source video is:

> Hermes knows you; the wiki knows your world.

That means Hermes memory stores personal preferences and durable facts, while the wiki stores broader external context: research, YouTube transcripts, meeting notes, project docs, and strategy.
