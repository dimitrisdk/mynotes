## Facts
- Google Search’s I/O 2026 updates: Gemini 3.5 Flash is now the default model for AI Mode worldwide; the search box has been upgraded with AI-powered suggestions that accept text, images, files, videos, or Chrome tabs; search agents (information agents, agentic booking, agentic coding) are launching this summer, with information agents for Google AI Pro/Ultra subscribers first, agentic booking for everyone in the U.S., and agentic coding for everyone this summer (early access for Pro/Ultra in the U.S.). Personal Intelligence (connecting Gmail, Photos, and soon Calendar) is expanding to ~200 countries/territories and 98 languages, no subscription required. AI Mode now serves >1 billion monthly users, with queries more than doubling each quarter.
- AI News Today – June 7, 2026: Apple is set to unveil at WWDC 2026 (June 8) a Siri rebuilt on a custom 1.2-trillion-parameter Google Gemini model (approx. $1B/year license), with multi-model extensions allowing users to choose ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude for Apple Intelligence (Gemini default). Trump and Sanders have converged on endorsing government equity stakes in AI firms, with Sanders proposing a 50% tax on OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI to fund a sovereign wealth fund. xAI has secured an 18-month US GSA OneGov contract for Grok 4 & Grok 4 Fast at $0.42 per agency, including dedicated engineering team and agency training.
- Sources: Google Blog (May 19, 2026), AI News Today (June 7, 2026), White House Executive Order on AI (June 2, 2026) [referenced in prior research], and Bloomberg/Mark Gurman via AI News Today.
