## Συμπέρασμα

Για τον Δημήτρη, τα καλύτερα side hustles δεν είναι “passive income” ή generic creator plays. Είναι μικρές, productized υπηρεσίες που αξιοποιούν ήδη υπάρχον AI/automation leverage:

1. **AI automation / agent workflows για μικρές επιχειρήσεις** — πιο άμεσο B2B revenue, premium pricing, ταιριάζει με Hermes/agent know-how.
2. **AI-assisted research briefs / market intelligence για founders & operators** — χαμηλό startup cost, υψηλή αξιοπιστία αν γίνει source-driven.
3. **Technical content repurposing για experts/companies** — scripts, summaries, LinkedIn/newsletter/video-to-article pipelines.
4. **Micro digital products/templates** — Obsidian/Notion/AI-agent operating systems, prompts, checklists, automations. Καλό ως secondary funnel, όχι πρώτο revenue bet.
5. **YouTube/newsletter niche authority** — μπορεί να δουλέψει, αλλά είναι πιο αργό και distribution-heavy.

Examples:

## Εκτίμηση

Avoid:
- generic “AI content agency”
- dropshipping
- print-on-demand without audience/design edge
- faceless YouTube as primary income plan
- generic Fiverr gigs competing on price

Best first bet:
- **Productized B2B AI Automation Sprint** with a narrow ICP.
- Secondary: research briefs as high-trust offer.
- Digital products only after the first 3–5 real client projects reveal repeated templates.

## Suggested 14-day validation plan

Day 1–2:
- Pick one ICP: e.g. coaches, small clinics, real-estate agents, local service businesses, agencies, accountants.
- Write one painful promise: “save 5 hours/week on X.”

Day 3–5:
- Build 2 demo workflows with screen recordings.
- Make a one-page offer: problem, before/after, price, timeline, guarantees.

Day 6–10:
- Contact 30 warm/local leads manually.
- Ask for 15-minute diagnosis, not “buy my AI thing”.

Day 11–14:
- Close 1 discounted pilot.
- Document time saved, before/after screenshots, testimonial.

Success threshold:
- At least 5 calls from 30 targeted messages OR 1 paid pilot. If not, change ICP/problem, not necessarily the whole thesis.
